Bee Safe Language of the Bees

I researched how honeybees communicate with the phenomenom of their abstract linguist capabilities through pheromones and the 'waggle dance.' This 800+ word blog is a collection of research across the web compiled into one page for evergreen content.

Bee Safe A Bee's Life

My largest work created is an article that describes every detail of a bee's life. All castes (worker, drone, and queen) are discussed in depth, being the only article of its kind in that matter.

Bee Safe Chimney Wasps

This blog was crafted in response to a customer need and several inquires on the topic of wasps in residential chimneys. Through the blog, steps to deal with the nest are provided. In addition, the blog informs readers of why a wasp nest is set up in a chimney and how that knowledge can be used to prevent further cases.
