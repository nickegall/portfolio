Research EnVision Utah

In tandem with my team of interns, we collected qualitative data on Utah drivers on their gas station choices and their sense of empowerment in regards to controlling pollution. I took this data and analyzed many insights to guide EnVision Utah on motivating Utahns.

Research Good Move Cafe

I researched the marketing and core competencies of Good Move Cafe (a local attraction) with three classmates. We noticed some key challenges to Good Move's strategies and recommended solutions backed by research to turn such challenging walls into a foundation for heightened success.

Research Theodore Global

Leading a team of interns as project manager, we gathered market research for points of entry for Theodore Global. This report and its insights is the culmination of the primary and secondary research we performed.
