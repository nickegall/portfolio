Marching Band

I have played the trumpet for 11 years, and I love marching band! Concert band is great with the difficult pieces--testing your skill and range--but there is an atmoshpere with marching band you cannot get otherwise.
With the BYU Cougar Marching Band, I served as the 1st trumpet squad leader during the season of 2020 and the section leader over 40 trumpets in the season of 2021!

Strategic & Competitve

I can get super competitve when playing games; I love playing strategically and coming out on top with just my wits. Fighting from behind is great when you win--feels great even if you lose, as long as a good fight was put up.
I enjoy playing board games that get super competitive (like Risk, but I also play games like Uno, Clue, and SCUM competitively), and I will also play video games to get that same kick; playing RTS and MOBA games are fun to test your skill and strategy against that of another's.
